My Life

I was born in Pensacola and lived my the majority of my life in Crestview. I graduated from Crestview High School in 2010. I then went to Troy University and participated in the Sound of the South marching band. I wasn't mature enough at the time so i decided to take my life a different direction. I joined the United States Air Force in 2012 and seperated in 2018. I then went to work for Lockheed Martin after my seperation. From there i went to work for the Federal Aviation Administration. At this point in my life I wanted a change of pace so i decided to go back to school for computer science.
