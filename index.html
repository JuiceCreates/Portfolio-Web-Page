Things To Bring

When people visit you inevitably will forget something necessary to have a good time on your vacation. A few mandatory items include sunscreen, a bathing suit or 4, flip flops, sun glasses, and a great attitude. These arent the ONLY things you will need but it will get you started on the right path!

Hotels

There are many hotels to stay at around the area. Visit the Hotels page to find the perfect stay for you during your trip to the Okaloosa County Area. You can stay near the beaches or a little more inland to hide away from the busy tourist areas.

Food

Destin Florida has some of the best seafood restaurants in the south eastern United States. Being right on the coast allows for some of the freshest seafood dishes you will ever taste! If you aren't a fan of seafood, there are plenty other local options to satisfy your taste-buds!

Activities

Northwest Florida has many different activities from water parks to fishing charters. There is a plethora of things to choose from to occupy your time while on your vacations. If shopping is your thing, you can check out a few of the outlets around the area that should have everything needed to fuel your fun trip to the Gulf Coast
